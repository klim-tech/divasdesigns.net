Soap Ingredients:
All soaps are handcrafted from vegetable glycerin soap base.  Any additives such as goats milk or exfoliating agents (loofa, pumice, etc.) are listed with the soap.  Soaps are scented with fragrance oils which are a combination of natural and synthetic ingredients.  Soaps with no added fragrance are marked "unscented."  (Some additives such as honey inpart a natural fragrance to the soap).  All colorants, glitters and micas are FDA certified cosmetic grade.  As with any product that comes in contact with your skin, if irritation occurs, discontinue use. 
All soaps are created and packaged with care by Diva!
